# Design Team

**Mandate:**

Design at Mindset supports all touchpoints of the customer's experience - from our website & marketing through to our product experience.&#x20;

* **The product experience:** We design buildable user experiences that are delightful and valuable for our customer base.&#x20;
* **Support the full brand experience:** Support the sales and marketing teams to ensure a seamless, brand experience across all touchpoints.
* **Move fast, think strategically, iterate often**: We balance user needs with business impact, working closely with product, engineering, and success teams to deliver what users need. We move fast, iterate often, and always keep people at the heart of what we do.

**Cadence:**

The design team meets twice a week for focused Design Reviews, and every other week for our Experience MOS sessions. We also join the weekly Product MOS to collaborate on cross-functional product issues, and participate in daily stand-ups with the Delivery team to stay aligned on execution.
