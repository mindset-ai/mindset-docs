# Sales & Marketing Team

**Mandate:**\
The Sales & Marketing team is responsible for driving revenue growth by generating demand, converting pipelines, and ensuring a seamless buying experience for our customers, and partners.

* Developing and executing sales and marketing strategies aligned with company OKRs.
* Removing friction in the buying process to accelerate deal velocity.
* Building and maintaining a predictable, scalable revenue engine.
* Strengthening market positioning through brand, messaging, and social proof.
* Optimising the full customer acquisition journey from awareness to closed-won.

**Cadence:**

* Weekly Sales & Marketing team meetings focused on pipeline growth, conversion rates, and overall sales and marketing metrics.
* MOS IDS meeting every other Thursday to resolve cross-functional issues.
