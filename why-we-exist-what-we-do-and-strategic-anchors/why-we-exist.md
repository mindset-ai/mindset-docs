# Why we exist

### To make technology more human&#x20;

We started with a first principle question; can we build technology that adapts to people, instead of people adapting to technology?&#x20;

We believe that technology can bridge the gap between ambition and ability. We believe technology has become cumbersome and frustrating over time, often harming instead of helping people.&#x20;

Yet when implemented correctly, technology unlocks information, freedom and creativity.&#x20;
