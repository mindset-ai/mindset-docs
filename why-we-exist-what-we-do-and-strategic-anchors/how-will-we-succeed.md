# How will we succeed?

Mindset has 2 core strategic anchors. These can change and evolve over time. We will review them every quarter.&#x20;

**Mindset will be the 'Ikea' of AI agents:**&#x20;

* This means building a modular product,&#x20;
* This means keeping everything beautifully simple
* This means our customers can decide to quickly configure things or customise to their hearts content&#x20;
* This means we are really easy to buy from, offering ready built examples and plenty of inspiration on what can be created (just like Ikea)

**We play where we can win:**&#x20;

* This means every project internally & every client project has known success metrics&#x20;
* This means we only work with people who share our values (no a\*\*holes)&#x20;
* This means we are for the many and not for the few &#x20;
* This means we will only operate in markets that we can experience triple digit growth and be the best in the world.&#x20;
