* Dependability – I take pride in being someone people can rely on. Whether it’s following through on a task, showing up prepared, or being a sounding board, consistency is at the heart of how I operate. Dependability builds trust, and trust builds great teams and relationships.&#x20;
* Empathy – I listen deeply, both to customers and colleagues. To me, empathy isn’t just being kind—it’s understanding people’s needs even when they can’t fully articulate them. That’s what enables me to deliver thoughtful, human-centred experiences.
* Growth mindset – I don’t believe in being static. I see every challenge as a chance to learn something new and get better—because “good enough” just isn’t. Whether it’s taking feedback or exploring new tools and methods, I’m always looking for that next 1%.

### Achilles heels

* Spontaneity – I thrive on structure and preparation. Put me on the spot and I may stumble, but give me a little space to think, and I’ll deliver something meaningful.

* Let me own it – I do my best work when I’m trusted to take initiative. Clear direction and autonomy go a long way. I don’t need to be micromanaged—I need the space to solve, improve, and grow.

* Let me learn by doing – I retain knowledge best through real-life application. Give me a challenge, and I’ll find a way—because learning in the moment is where I thrive.
