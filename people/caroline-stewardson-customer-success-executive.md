# Caroline Stewardson - Customer Success Executive

I’m naturally inquisitive and enjoy understanding how a product creates real value and impact for customers. I live on the south coast (with my husband and daughter) where I enjoy spending time in the countryside and swimming in the sea (in the spring / summer, I’ll leave cold water swimming to the brave!). Being outdoors helps me reset and bring renewed energy into my work environment.

### Roles

Team Member of the [Customer Success Team](https://mos.mindset.ai/teams/customer-success-team)

### Personal Values

* **Inquisitive** – I love asking questions, exploring challenges and expanding my knowledge to improve both my work and the customer experience.&#x20;
* **Dependable** – I take pride in being someone others can rely on. When I commit to something, I see it through.
* **Empathy** – Customer success is about people first. I take time to listen, understand the customers needs and ensure that every decision is rooted in what’s in their best interests.&#x20;
* **Diligence** – I believe that success is often in the details. I take a thorough, thoughtful approach, ensuring that customers always receive a high level of service and support.

### Achilles heels

* **Detail-Oriented to a Fault** - I enjoy getting into the details, occasionally too much. I’m learning to appreciate that a broader approach can often be more effective.&#x20;
* **Perfectionism** – I set high standards for myself, which drives quality, but it can sometimes slow me down when “good enough” would be the better option.

### How to get the best out of me

* **Encourage my inquisitiveness** – I thrive when I can explore, ask questions, and develop a deep understanding of the product and customer needs.&#x20;
* **Be open and direct** – I appreciate transparency, clear communication, and a shared commitment to solving problems.
