# Rhea Arora - Technical Product Marketing Manager

Curious by nature and ambitious by design, I’ve taken a scenic route through the tech world. Armed with a software engineering degree, I dabbled in growth, sales, digital marketing, and product marketing - all in pursuit of figuring out what I truly love. Turns out, it’s the magic of building great products with small, nimble teams where I get to wear many hats (and occasionally switch them mid-meeting). I thrive in environments where no two days are the same, and where my to-do list has sub-lists.

Outside of work, I’m a food lover with a sweet tooth so fierce it needs to be tamed at the gym (it’s all about balance :) ). I’m a devoted fiction reader - reality has enough plot twists - and a huge dog enthusiast.
