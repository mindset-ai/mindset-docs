# MOS Handbook

### About MOS

This handbook is the **definitive guide** to how we work at Mindset AI. It documents our core processes, explains why they exist, and serves as a transparent resource for anyone working with or considering joining us.

We call this system **MOS (Mindset Operating System)**—the foundation of how we run the company. Think of this handbook as its user manual.

Explore the sections below to learn more. **We believe in full transparency, so read carefully—this is how we do things.**

Think of MOS as the company’s operating system and this handbook as its user manual. It’s a living system that evolves as we do, and improvements are always welcome. Just be sure to follow the agreed-upon process for updates.

### **Why does MOS exist?**

To understand MOS, you first need to know our **Vision** and **Mission**:

> **Vision:** Leveraging AI to bridge the gap between human ability and human ambition.
>
> **Mission**: Building a sustainable, creative, fulfilled and diverse global company using groundbreaking thinking and structures to positively impact the lives of everyone we work with and those who rely on our platform.

MOS is how we bring this mission to life—it’s the **user manual** for how Mindset AI operates. It defines how we run our company and organize ourselves to achieve our goals.

### Why is MOS public?

Transparency is a core value at Mindset AI. By openly documenting our ways of working, values, and priorities, we make a statement about who we are and how we operate.

### Who is MOS for? <a href="#who-is-mos-for" id="who-is-mos-for"></a>

If you’re reading this, MOS is for you. It’s for anyone who works with or is considering working with us—whether you’re a **customer, partner, investor, employee, or prospective hire**. It’s here to help you understand **who we are, why we exist, and what we believe in**.
